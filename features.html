System Capabilities

The AI Real Estate Powerhouse

GetChurnShield is not just a chatbot. It is a complete pipeline automation platform designed specifically for the dynamics, objections, and logistics of the Nigerian property market.

Omnichannel Comment Scraper

Connect your Instagram organic posts, Facebook ads, and Twitter investor threads. The engine tracks comments on all channels simultaneously, matching user accounts to initiate instant direct conversations.

WhatsApp "Ghost-Agent"

Bypasses generic messages in favor of standard WhatsApp phone number chats. The agent replies with human typing indicators and friendly, localized phrasing to establish early customer trust.

Smart Smartphone Alerts

No complex log-ins required. When a lead schedules an inspection and confirms they meet pricing metrics, the AI texts the qualification details and a WhatsApp takeover link directly to your agent's smartphone.

Auto-Fallback Emails

If a prospect doesn't answer on WhatsApp or drops off, our Pro/Ultimate plans trigger an automated fallback email drip offering estate flyers and neighborhood summaries to keep them engaged.

Autonomous Voice Dialing

Exclusive to the Ultimate tier. The engine automatically initiates outbound phone follow-ups to inactive database leads, pitching your latest land launches and scheduling site tours using synthetic voices.

Webhook CRM Syncer

Connect GetChurnShield with Salesforce, HubSpot, or custom agency backends. Send qualification details, WhatsApp transcripts, lead metrics, and transaction values automatically in real time.

Built for Complex Real Estate Logistics

We load our AI model with full parameters of your property inventory sheets.

Local Property Objections

Our conversational models are pre-trained to navigate questions about Nigeria's unique title classifications (e.g., Excision, Deed of Assignment, Gazette, Certificate of Occupancy, Governor's Consent) and property objections like development levies, drainage facilities, electricity guarantees, and estate security.

Custom Setup Persona Matching

In our Pro and Ultimate plans, we ingest samples of your actual sales script records and consultant chats. We fine-tune the AI's persona parameters so its output tone matches your exact branding guidelines (whether formal executive corporate or friendly advisory realtor).

Secure Verification Filters

To protect your sales team's schedule, the AI filters out casual browsers and spam. It uses specific qualifying prompts (e.g. asking for preferred downpayment sizes and target purchase dates) to verify lead intent before notifying staff.
