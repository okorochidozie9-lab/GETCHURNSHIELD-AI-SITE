The Automation Blueprint

How GetChurnShield Works

Traditional real estate agencies take an average of 4.5 hours to respond to a listing comment. By then, the client is cold. GetChurnShield closes this gap completely, taking a lead from comment status to WhatsApp qualification in less than 3 seconds. Here is how we do it.

STAGE 01

24/7 Intent Scraping

The moment an organic reel goes viral or an Instagram/Facebook ad drops, our scrapers monitor every incoming comment. Instead of generic bots that answer on the post, our engine analyzes user intent using semantic algorithms.

  • Trigger Phrases: Detects keywords like "How much?", "Where is this located?", "PM price", "Is it still available?"
  • Instant ID Match: Pulls the commenter's profile data to extract their active account details.
  • Speed Metrics: Zero latency scraping completes the extraction in 2.8 seconds on average.

STAGE 02

Ghost-Agent Nurturing

Instead of sending a generic direct message (DM) that gets buried, our engine starts a custom WhatsApp dialog. The AI represents itself as a senior consultant from your agency, using personalized details of the listing sheets to reply.

  • No Bot Clichés: The chat uses real-world pauses and natural vocabulary, answering objections without robotic terminology.
  • Localized Context: Tailored specifically for Nigerian real estate, validating title structures, C of O, and area amenities (e.g. clean water, security).
  • Budget Qualification: Naturally extracts deposit abilities and purchasing timelines before notifying human staff.
STAGE 03

Live Closing Handoff

We don't clutter your inbox with unvetted lists. The AI only sends a smartphone notification to your team when a client explicitly requests a site inspection and verifies their budget capabilities.

  • SMS & Push Alerts: Sent directly to your sales consultant's phone with a summary dossier.
  • One-Click Takeover: Click the WhatsApp link in the alert to jump directly into the conversation.
  • Closed Loop Analytics: Log closed deals back to your dashboard to retrain the AI persona.
